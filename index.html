Gantt charts (waterfall charts)

Planning

Plan for reality not fantasy

Only plan what you need to

Both for project and sprints
Only plan out the next move in detail that will provide value to your customer
Roadmaps are good and strategic goals make sense, but they can change.

Break your work into stories

Writing a story is about determining value

As a (who)

I need (what)

So that (why)

Business requirements and test criteria should fall out of the story.

Stories should be small

Independent

Negotiable

Valuable

Estimable

Small

Testable

INVEST
Helps everyone to know exactly what needs to be done.
You need to break down the stories if they don't fit into this.

Use relative sizing

People suck at absolute sizing

People are good at relative sizing (story points)

Example of going to get groceries
Example of relative sizing (t-shirt sizes, fibonacci numbers)
